About the model

Stocks are scored 0–100 using four factors:

  • Value (35%) — P/E, P/B ratios, dividend yield. Lower P/E and P/B = higher score.
  • Momentum (35%) — Price momentum over 1m, 3m, 6m. Positive momentum = higher score.
  • RSI (15%) — Relative Strength Index. Oversold (<30) = potential buy; overbought (>70) = caution.
  • Volume (15%) — Increasing volume suggests growing interest.

Signals: STRONG BUY ≥70 · BUY ≥60 · HOLD ≥50 · WEAK <50
